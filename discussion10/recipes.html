Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

I learned how to make chicken in the air fryer from Youtube, but I then tried seasoning chicken myself. It isnâ€™t anything spectacular by any means, but it is quick and easy.


Recipe Ingredients:

  • Chicken Thighs (2-4 pieces)
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Blackened Chicken seasoning
  • Olive oil

Recipe Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, place 2-4 pieces of chicken thighs in
  2. Add the salt, paprika, blackened chicken seasoning by eyeballing it
  3. Mix the seasoning in with the chicken
  4. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees for 3 minutes
  5. Rub some olive oil on the chicken
  6. Place in air fryer for 22 minutes
  7. Flip at 12 minutes
  8. Cook for remainder of time, until the temperature is 165 degrees

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Easiest sandwich and delicous!


Recipe Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread
  • knife

Recipe Steps:

  1. Take two slices of bread out
  2. Using the knife, apply peanut butter onto one slice.
  3. Wipe the knife off
  4. Apply jelly to the other slice with knife
  5. Put sandwich together
